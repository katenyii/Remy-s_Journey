Remy

Remy is a little mouse with big dreams of becoming a famous chef. He has an extraordinary sense of smell and an indomitable spirit.

Alfredo Linguini

A clumsy young man who works in the kitchen of Gusteau's. Together with Remy, he forms a formidable team to create extraordinary dishes.

Anton Ego

Anton Ego is a renowned and feared food critic from the film Ratatouille. He is known as "The Grim Eater" for his scathing food reviews, capable of "killing" restaurants with his harsh criticism.

Chef Skinner

An angry and manipulative chef tries to take over Gusteau's restaurant and opposes Remy and Linguini's presence.
